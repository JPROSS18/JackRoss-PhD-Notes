#### Definition:

If $f: X \to X$ is an invertible map and $x \in X$ a point such that $\lim_{n \to \infty} f^{-n} = a$ and $\lim_{n \to \infty} f^{n} = b$, where $a \neq b$, then $x$ is said to be **heteroclinic*** to $a$ and $b$. If $a = b$, then $x$ is a **homoclinic** point of $a$.
